# Service Pricing

## Standard Plans

### Starter Plan
- **Price**: $299/month
- **Best for**: Small businesses, freelancers
- **Includes**:
  - Up to 5 team members
  - 100 GB storage
  - Email support
  - Basic analytics
  - 99.5% uptime SLA

### Professional Plan
- **Price**: $799/month
- **Best for**: Growing businesses
- **Includes**:
  - Up to 25 team members
  - 500 GB storage
  - Priority email & chat support
  - Advanced analytics & reporting
  - 99.9% uptime SLA
  - API access
  - Custom integrations (up to 5)

### Enterprise Plan
- **Price**: Custom pricing (starting at $2,499/month)
- **Best for**: Large organizations
- **Includes**:
  - Unlimited team members
  - Unlimited storage
  - 24/7 phone & email support
  - Dedicated account manager
  - 99.99% uptime SLA
  - Full API access
  - Unlimited custom integrations
  - White-label options
  - On-premise deployment available
