# Frequently Asked Questions

## General Questions

### What is your service?
We provide a comprehensive business automation platform that helps companies streamline their operations, improve customer engagement, and scale efficiently. Our platform integrates with your existing tools and workflows to provide seamless automation.

### Who is this service for?
Our service is designed for businesses of all sizes, from small startups to large enterprises. We have plans tailored to different needs and budgets.

### Do you offer a free trial?
Yes! We offer a 14-day free trial on all plans with full feature access. No credit card is required to start your trial.

### What happens after my trial ends?
After your trial, you can choose to upgrade to a paid plan. If you don't upgrade, your account will be downgraded to our free tier with limited features. You won't lose any of your data.
