# Refund and Cancellation Policy

## Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a **14-day money-back guarantee** on all new purchases.

### Eligibility
- Applies to first-time customers only
- Must be requested within 14 days of initial purchase
- Does not apply to renewals
- Does not apply to custom professional services

### How to Request
1. Email support@company.com with subject "Refund Request"
2. Include your account email and reason for refund
3. We'll process within 5 business days
4. Refund issued to original payment method

## Cancellation Policy

### Monthly Plans
- Cancel anytime, no questions asked
- Cancellation takes effect at end of current billing period
- Retain access until end of paid period
- No partial refunds for unused time
- Can reactivate within 30 days without data loss

### Annual Plans
- Cancel anytime
- No refund for unused months (except within 14-day guarantee period)
- Can continue using until end of annual term
- Will not auto-renew after cancellation

### How to Cancel
1. Log into your account
2. Navigate to Settings > Billing
3. Click "Cancel Subscription"
4. Confirm cancellation
5. Receive confirmation email

Or contact support@company.com

## Downgrades

### Process
- Downgrades take effect at next billing cycle
- Current features available until downgrade date
- Prorated credit NOT issued for downgrades
- Data may be subject to new plan limits

### Data Retention
When downgrading:
- Data exceeding new plan limits will be archived
- Archived data retained for 90 days
- Can upgrade to regain access
- Can export data before downgrade

## Upgrades

### Process
- Upgrades take effect immediately
- Prorated credit applied for remaining time on old plan
- New features available instantly
- No data migration needed

### Billing
- Charged immediately for prorated amount
- Next full billing at regular interval from upgrade date

## Pausing Service

### Enterprise Plans Only
- Can pause service for up to 3 months
- Pay 20% of monthly fee during pause
- Data retained during pause
- Reactivation within 24 hours

### Not Available For
- Starter and Professional plans
- Cannot pause during contract minimum term

## Force Majeure

Service may be suspended without refund for:
- Natural disasters
- Government orders
- War or terrorism
- Pandemics
- Other events beyond our control

We will make commercially reasonable efforts to restore service.

## Violations and Termination

### We Reserve the Right to Terminate
Immediate termination without refund for:
- Violation of Terms of Service
- Fraudulent activity
- Abusive behavior toward staff
- Illegal use of platform
- Excessive resource usage beyond plan limits

### Process
1. Warning issued (except for severe violations)
2. 7-day correction period (if applicable)
3. Account suspension
4. Permanent termination if not resolved

### Data Access
- 14 days to export data after termination
- After 14 days, data permanently deleted

## Disputes

### Billing Disputes
- Must be raised within 60 days of charge
- Email billing@company.com with details
- Investigation completed within 10 business days
- Good-faith errors will be refunded

### Chargebacks
- Contact us first to resolve issue
- Chargebacks result in immediate account suspension
- Account reinstated after chargeback withdrawn
- $50 administrative fee for chargebacks

## Pro-Rata Refunds

We do NOT offer pro-rata refunds except:
- Service outage exceeding SLA (credit issued)
- Termination by us without cause
- Billing errors
